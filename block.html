Welcome to B.L.O.C.K

Have you ever been bullied by a "friend", family member, co-worker, or someone online?

Well we can help, at B.L.O.C.K we incourage people that have been bullied to speak out so we can help manage stressful situations.

If you didn't know, B.L.O.C.K means Bulling Hurts Lives for other people to Collect Knowledge and mindfulness also the meaning behind is blocking bullies and helping overcome you bully past.

We have alot of ways that you can help you with your bullying problems. For example, you could tell a person that you are getting bullied by a person.

You are not alone

You are not the only one that's being bullied. There are many people that are being bullied. It might be because of anything, but no matter what do not bully anyone for any reason.

Something that you can do if you see someone getting bullied is caring for them or even standing up for them. please remember that you can always do something.
